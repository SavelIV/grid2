I am a Web-Developer
Who Cares.

I am trying to be helping hand in the world of Web Development for my clients. Cooperatively with my clients, we move towards a successful solution. And this is how the efficient working process should flow. Being a fan of the “think-before-you-act” approach, I prefer to spend some time figuring out a bigger picture of your task/problem before diving into coding. This will prevent you from lots of redos in the future, thus saving our time and your money.

In my work I strive for the following principles:

- End product needs to solve a problem

- My client should feel safe and involved in the process (seeing intermediate results, getting instructions, etc)

- To be independent as a developer and free my clients' brains from thinking and caring too much about things that are my responsibility.

Thank You!
